Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2025

Thank you for using Scam Link Defender. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data when you use our Chrome Extension.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Scam Link Defender does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following information:

  • Personally identifiable information (PII)
  • Financial or payment information
  • Health or biometric data
  • Browsing history or page content
  • IP addresses, device IDs, or geolocation
  • Authentication credentials (e.g., passwords)
  • Any data entered by users in forms or websites

2. Local Processing Only

All functionality runs locally in your browser. The extension analyzes URLs and page content in real-time to identify and warn against phishing or scam links. No data is sent to external servers.

3. Permissions Used

We only request the permissions required to perform our single purpose:

  • activeTab, tabs, webNavigation, webRequest, scripting: To scan pages and identify scam links.
  • storage: To save user preferences locally.
  • Host permissions: To scan across websites.

4. No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer any information (because none is collected) to third parties, advertisers, or analytics providers.

5. Compliance

This extension complies with the Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies and privacy laws including GDPR and CCPA.
